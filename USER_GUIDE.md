## Getting Started

### What is Snapocado?
Snapocado is a mobile application designed to help avocado farmers and enthusiasts detect diseases, pests, and leaf conditions in real-time using AI-powered image recognition. The app uses TensorFlow Lite models to analyze photos of avocado fruits, leaves, and trees to provide immediate diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

### Installation & Setup
1. Download and install the Snapocado app from your device's app store
2. Grant the app permission to access your device's camera and photos
3. Launch the app and navigate through the home screen

### Supported Detection Models
The app includes three specialized detection models:

| Model | Purpose | Detectable Classes |
|-------|---------|-------------------|
| **Fruit Model** | Detect avocado fruit diseases | Healthy fruit, Anthracnose, Scab |
| **Leaf Model** | Detect leaf diseases and pests | Healthy, Anthracnose leaf, Mites, Powdery mildew |
| **Tree Model** | Detect tree-level pests | Borer |

## Capture Detection

### How to Use Capture
1. **Select Detection Type**
   - Choose which part of the avocado plant you want to analyze:
     - **Fruit** - For detecting fruit diseases (scab, anthracnose)
     - **Leaf** - For detecting leaf conditions and pests (mites, anthracnose, powdery mildew)
     - **Tree** - For detecting tree-level pests (borer)

2. **Take a Photo**
   - Click the **camera button** to take a live photo
   - Ensure good lighting and clear visibility of the subject
   - Hold the camera steady for best results

3. **View Results**
   - The app processes the image using AI
   - Results appear instantly showing:
     - **Detected condition** (disease/pest name)
     - **Confidence score** (reliability of detection)
     - **Status indicator** (Healthy/Disease/Pest)

### Best Practices for Capture
- **Lighting**: Use natural daylight; avoid shadows and glare
- **Distance**: Keep the camera 6-12 inches from the subject
- **Angle**: Capture the clearest view of affected areas
- **Background**: Plain backgrounds work best
- **Multiple shots**: Take several photos for better accuracy

### How to Use Upload

## Treatments & Management Guide

### Accessing Treatments
1. Tap **Treatments** from the main menu
2. Choose between **Mga Sakit (Diseases)** or **Mga Peste (Pests)**

### Disease Treatments Covered

#### 1. **Scab**
- **Symptoms**: Brown, corky spots on fruits and leaves
- **Treatment Steps**: Includes pruning, sanitation, fungicide application
- **Key Actions**: Remove affected fruits, improve airflow, apply copper fungicide every 2 weeks
- **Prevention**: Use disease-resistant varieties, maintain orchard cleanliness

#### 2. **Anthracnose (Fruit)**
- **Symptoms**: Dark circular spots on developing fruits
- **Treatment Steps**: Remove dead plant material, apply fungicide to developing fruits
- **Key Actions**: Spray copper fungicide during fruiting stage, prune for better airflow
- **Prevention**: Early detection and prompt treatment crucial

#### 3. **Anthracnose (Leaf)**
- **Symptoms**: Dark spots on leaves, spotty appearance
- **Treatment Steps**: Prune affected foliage, improve air circulation
- **Key Actions**: Remove infected leaves and twigs, rake debris, spray fungicide every 2 weeks during rainy season
- **Prevention**: Maintain dry conditions, regular pruning

#### 4. **Powdery Mildew**
- **Symptoms**: White powdery coating on leaves
- **Treatment Steps**: Apply sulfur fungicide or homemade baking soda spray
- **Key Actions**: Spray weekly for 3-4 weeks, remove affected leaves, improve sunlight exposure
- **Prevention**: Maintain good tree health and proper nutrition

### Pest Management Covered

#### 1. **Persea Mites (Spider Mites)**
- **Symptoms**: Yellow spots on leaves, fine webbing, tiny moving dots
- **Treatment Steps**: Release beneficial predatory mites (biological control)
- **Key Actions**: Monitor leaf undersides, release Neoseiulus californicus when 50-75% of leaves affected
- **Prevention**: Maintain proper irrigation and tree health
- **Important**: Do not use chemical pesticides after releasing beneficial mites

#### 2. **Fruit Borers**
- **Symptoms**: Small holes in fruits, internal larvae damage
- **Treatment Steps**: Collect and destroy affected fruits, maintain sanitation
- **Key Actions**: Remove fallen fruits and leaves, prune for better airflow, spray organic insecticides (Neem oil or Bt) every 7-10 days during fruiting
- **Prevention**: Regular monitoring and sanitation crucial

### How to Use Treatment Guides
1. **Select Category**: Choose Diseases or Pests
2. **Choose Treatment**: Tap on the specific condition
3. **Read Overview**: Understand the condition and approach
4. **Expand Details**: Click "Buong Gabay sa Gamot" (Full Treatment Guide)
5. **Follow Steps**: Review the complete step-by-step instructions
6. **Take Action**: Implement treatments in your orchard

## Troubleshooting

### Detection Not Working

#### Problem: Camera Permission Denied
- **Solution**: Go to Settings → Apps → Snapocado → Permissions → Enable Camera and Photos

#### Problem: Detection Taking Too Long
- **Solution**: 
  - Check internet connection (if cloud processing is enabled)
  - Ensure sufficient device storage
  - Close other apps to free up memory
  - Restart the app

#### Problem: Low Confidence Results
- **Solution**:
  - Retake photo with better lighting
  - Ensure subject is clearly visible
  - Get closer to the affected area (6-12 inches)
  - Remove shadows and glare

#### Problem: Wrong Detection Result
- **Solution**:
  - The model may need a clearer image
  - Ensure you selected the correct detection type (Fruit/Leaf/Tree)
  - Try taking multiple photos from different angles
  - Verify the condition matches the description

### Storage & Performance

#### Problem: App Running Slow
- **Solution**:
  - Clear app cache: Settings → Apps → Snapocado → Storage → Clear Cache
  - Delete old history entries
  - Free up device storage space
  - Restart your device

#### Problem: Unable to Save Photos
- **Solution**:
  - Check available device storage
  - Grant Storage permission to the app
  - Delete unnecessary files from device

### History Issues

#### Problem: History Not Saving
- **Solution**:
  - Grant Storage permission to the app
  - Ensure app is not in read-only mode
  - Try clearing app cache
  - Restart the application

#### Problem: Cannot View Saved Images
- **Solution**:
  - Verify photos still exist in device storage
  - Check file format compatibility
  - Reimport images if necessary

## Best Practices for Optimal Results

### Before Taking Photos
1. **Plan Your Shot**
   - Identify the clearest affected area
   - Avoid cluttered backgrounds
   - Check lighting conditions

2. **Prepare Your Device**
   - Clean camera lens
   - Ensure good battery level
   - Close unnecessary apps

### During Photo Capture
1. **Positioning**
   - Hold camera 6-12 inches from subject
   - Keep camera steady (use both hands)
   - Ensure focus on the target area

2. **Lighting**
   - Use natural daylight when possible
   - Avoid harsh shadows
   - Don't backlighting into the sun
   - Overcast days often provide ideal lighting

3. **Composition**
   - Frame the affected area clearly
   - Include some healthy tissue for comparison
   - Minimize distracting background elements

### After Detection
1. **Review Results**
   - Read the full treatment guide
   - Note the confidence score
   - Cross-reference with visual symptoms

2. **Take Action**
   - Implement recommended treatments
   - Monitor the area for changes
   - Document with follow-up photos

3. **Maintain Records**
   - Keep history for tracking progress
   - Date your observations
   - Track which treatments work best

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I use photos from the internet?
**A:** Yes, you can upload any avocado fruit, leaf, or tree photo. However, photos taken from your own orchard will typically yield more relevant results.

### Q: How accurate is the detection?
**A:** The AI models are trained on thousands of images and achieve 85%+ accuracy. However, lighting, image clarity, and angle affect results. Always verify with visual inspection.

### Q: Does the app require internet?
**A:** For native Android builds, detection runs entirely on-device and does not require internet. Web versions may require cloud processing.

### Q: Can I export my history?
**A:** Yes, tap on any detection and use the share button to export via email or messaging apps.

### Q: How often should I use the app?
**A:** Weekly monitoring is recommended during growing season, more frequently if you notice issues.

### Q: Can I use this instead of professional diagnosis?
**A:** The app is a helpful tool for early detection and initial assessment. For critical decisions, consult with local agricultural extension services.

### Q: What if I get a "No detection" result?
**A:** This means the model didn't find a disease/pest with sufficient confidence. Either there's no issue, the image quality needs improvement, or try the correct detection type.

### Q: How do I delete all my history?
**A:** Open History → tap the delete/trash icon → select "Clear All" → confirm deletion.
